My Story

My entire family originated from Russia and they came to the United States in 1991. I was the first person in my family to be born in the US, commonly known as first generation, on November 4th, 2000 in Denver Colorado. I moved to San Diego California, where I was eventually raised, when I was five years old because my mom wanted to live in a warm place for once in her life. She fell in love with San Diego and never left.

I played football and rugby in high school without any previous knowledge of either sport to try something new and make friends. I quickly gained a love for both sports and ended up playing them all throughout high school. My background in sports bred my present day passion for general fitness and healthy nutrition.

I was borderline addicted to video games growing up which lead to my eventual desire to study Computer Science in college. After taking Calculus I and Introduction to Computer Architecture, I realized that I was only cut out to focus on the coding aspect of the degree. That's when I decided to change my major to Software Development. Now I live out my days coding and learning how to properly design software which can be used in a professional environment.
